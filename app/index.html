Japan Rocks!

A long time ago in another corner of the world I was a Japanese Major on a small island know as Oahu. It was always my dream to go live in Japan. My first time there I went to visit a friend who I had met while she was traveling to Hawaii. She let me stay at her sisters apartment and we had a great time visiting all the spots in Tokyo.

Why does Japan Rock?

Japan is a great place to visit throughout the year, however the best time is during the Spring when the flowers blossom, and the Fall when the leaves change colors. There are many sites to visit from modern Tokyo, to historical Kyoto. Their are endless surprises ahead of you as you are amazed by the various strange gadgets found in the everyday home and the simplicity of blending nature with religion found in the temples and shrines.
